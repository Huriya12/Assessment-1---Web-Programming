The Old-Fashioned Country Pancake Recipe

A warm and comforting way to begin your day; made with ingredients that can already be found in your home.


♠ Ingredients ♠

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

♠ Steps ♠

  1. In a saucepan, combine heavy cream and sugar.
  2. In another mixing bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter, honey, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients until well-blended.
  4. Warm pan over gentle fire or medium-heat.
  5. Grease pan with butter.
  6. Pour ladleful of pancake batter onto pan, forming small, round pancakes.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, and the edges appear set, for about 2-3 minutes.
  8. Flip pancake on the other side; cook until golden brown, for about 2-3 minutes.
  9. Once cooked, remove pan from heat.
  10. Stack pancakes on top of each other.
  11. Garnish with berries and dust lightly with powdered sugar (optional)
  12. Drizzle with maple-syrup or honey.
  13. And... enjoy.
