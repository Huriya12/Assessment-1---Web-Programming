The Ultimate Hershey's Brownie Recipe

An oldie but goodie brownie recipe that never goes wrong nor out of style; made with ingredients that can already be found in your home.


♠ Ingredients ♠

  • ½ cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup Hershey's Cocoa powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup chocolate chips(optional)

♠ Steps ♠

  1. Preheat oven to 350F, and while at it, grease a baking pan of your choice with some oil or butter.
  2. In a mixing bowl, blend oil, sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, and beat well with spoon... or fork.
  4. In another mixing bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients until well-blended.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Spread batter into a greased baking pan.
  8. Bake at 350F for 20 to 25 minutes, or until brownies begin to pull away from edges of the pan.
  9. Once baked, let it cool down for some time.
  10. And... enjoy.
