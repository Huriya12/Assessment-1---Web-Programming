The Lemon Blossom Posset Recipe

A bright, citrusy treat for a sunny afternoon; made with ingredients that can already be found in your home.


♠ Ingredients ♠

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 zest lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

♠ Steps ♠

  1. In a saucepan, combine heavy cream and sugar.
  2. Heat over medium-heat, until sugar dissolves and the mixture simmers gently
  3. Let mixture simmer for about 3 minutes, with frequent stirring.
  4. Add citrus.
  5. Remove saucepan from heat.
  6. Stir in freshly squeezed lemon juice and lemon zest.
  7. Pour mixture into molds.
  8. Let posset cool in room temperature.
  9. Cover and chill psset in refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight, until firm.
  10. Garnish with mint leaves, edible flowers, or lemon zest.
  11. And... enjoy.
