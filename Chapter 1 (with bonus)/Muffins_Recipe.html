The Perfect Chocolate Chip Muffin Recipe

A sweet and simple treat, bursting with rich chocolatey goodness and a warm, comforting feeling with every bite; made with ingredients that can already be found in your home.


♠ Ingredients ♠

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • ½ cup sour cream or plain yogurt

♠ Steps ♠

  1. Preheat oven to 350F, and while at it, grease a muffin tin with some oil or butter and line with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, and beat well with spoon... or fork.
  4. In another mixing bowl, whisk together melted butter, eggs, milk, sour cream or plain yogurt, and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients until well-blended.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Spoon batter onto muffin tins.
  8. Spread batter into a greased baking pan.
  9. Bake at 350F for 18 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the muffin comes out clean.
  10. Once baked, let it cool down for some time.
  11. And... enjoy.
